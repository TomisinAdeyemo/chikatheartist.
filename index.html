ABOUT ME

“Art is how I express
what I don’t have words for — yet.”

I’m Chika, and I make art,
super original — I know.

Since i could remember creativity has always been something that has driven me, to an extent i feel like it was all i could look forward to in high school, weather it was singing,

Crocheting or making any art in general. when i started drawing i knew it was something that would become an escape for me and a different but freeing mode of expression

Multiple sketches, different modes of art expressions too many opinions, and the freedom to create new pieces no one thought they needed, and now i get to share it with you.

Send a Request

Submit a Request — Fill out the commission form or contact me directly with your concept, size, and style preferences.

Consultation & Quote — I’ll review your request and provide a price estimate, timeline, and any design suggestions.

Deposit & Start — Once the deposit (typically 50%) is received, I’ll begin your project and send progress updates.

Final Approval — You’ll have a chance to review the completed work before final payment.

Delivery — Once approved, I’ll deliver your artwork in the requested format (digital file or physical print). Depending on your location, delivery may take 1–2 weeks to 6–8 weeks.
